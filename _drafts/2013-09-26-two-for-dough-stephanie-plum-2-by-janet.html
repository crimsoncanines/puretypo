ebook, 304 pages


Published July 15th 1999 by Scribner (first published 1996)

I am officially a fan of Janet Evanovich!! absolutely in love with her writings!!

Two for the dough is the second book in the stephnie plum series and third one I have read by the author. The story is about Stephnie Plum who is independent and broke. And she merely has enough money to buy a donut for herself and her hamster. The first book in the series is about her quitting a series of jobs and finally landing up in the whole business of bounty hunter. She initially thinks it is not very difficult to turn in car thieves and law breakers. but the job has many surprises for her. In the first book she is merely getting the feel of the job. and also meeting her expenses, somehow!!

In the second book the job gets really serious for her since all her money is gone and she needs a big assignment to meet the needs. She eyes on the biggest assignment available then. Which is to bring is Kenny Mancuso, Joe morelli's cousin. Does not sound difficult but in reality it is getting close to impossible. Kenny is a retard criminal who enjoys cutting body parts of people and mailing them to Stephnie. 

The storyline is very interesting. The characters are very powerful and keep you alive. Granny Mazur takes the cake away this time too. I absolutely love her!! She is a daring, fashionable and fun. Absolute entertainer. And how can i miss joe morelli. If i had not already read the tenth book in the series I would be hating him all the way. But he's a good guy, trust me!!

The book is an absolute entertainer. The writing is simple and witty. Its packed with fun and thrill. Please go ahead and read it. You are going to love Janet evanovich after this.
