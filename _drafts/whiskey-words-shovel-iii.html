Love me to the bone
Beneath the nerves
Beyond my mistakes

Love me entirely
Or not at all

This collection of poetry by R.H. Stine is here is to make a statement. The poet is here is revive the long lost love back to life. It is how love should be. Healthy and lively. Any other kind if unacceptable. The poems are from will touch your soul and heal.

Cover Page
The cover page is very simple as poetry collections usually are. It is a no-nonsense, straight to the point cover. The poet talks about heart as it is and that is what the cover page portrays.
Characters
The poets talks about that girl, his girl, the woman who was strong, the man who loved and other people and of course, himself. The poetry is written from different points of views. There is just one thing common in all of them. They are, were or have been in love with someone.
