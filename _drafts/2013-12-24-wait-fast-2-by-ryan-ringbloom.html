200 pages
Published October 28th 2013 by BookBaby

I received this book in exchange of an honest review.

Ryan Ringbloom does it again. this is my second book by the author and i must say i am more than impressed. this author is slowly climbing up to my favorite romance writer's list. here is what i think of the book. 

Cover-page:  cover page again is very subtle and well thought over. Better than the last book but I would still say “too much of photoshopping”. Apart from this it is well and good.


Characters: the characters are very well thought over in the book. the story carry forwards the tale of Tessa and her resolve to find the right guy before she gives up her virginity. She and Ashley are best friends now. Tessa volunteers to counsel at a summer camp where she meets a new girl Cassie. They hate each other from the time Cassie forces her brother to kiss Tessa. But things get better and they become good friends. The male characters include Tucker, Mark and Sawyer. You are sure to fall in love with Tessa and Tucker.


Story line: the story continues from the first book where Tessa tries to get along with Kent but cannot go further than being good friends. She gives up and goes to volunteer at a summer camp as a counselor. Things turn out to be good for her when she finds Tucker, her possible love interest. She also meets other people including Cassie and Mark. The story is well thought over and well written.



Concept: concept is interesting but not very different. After prom teenagers go to summer camp to take a break before they start college. But well played out I must say.



Language: the language is very simple and to the point. You would probably hate the first person writing but shall get over it eventually.



Good points: the book is very well written. The characters make you fall in love with them. It is amazing how Ryan portrays every teenage character different where you might think oh! They are all the same! 



Bad points: the first person narration may come across as irritating but it did not bother me much especially after reading the first book. This is author’s style then who am I to question! Also in the beginning chapters you might have a problem decoding who is hooking up with whom when you finally know the couples in the story.



Overall: I loved the second book as much as I did the first one. I shall definitely keep a track of the upcoming books by the author. The new one is releasing in 2014. But a date has not been let out yet. I am excited because this time it is going to be Josh’s story! I would love to see how Ryan turns him into a good guy after all the wrongs he has done in his life!! Tough task I must say!!
