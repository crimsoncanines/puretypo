This Pulitzer Prize-winning author bid us her final goodbye, and we are a little heartbroken (no, make that 'very'). Harper Lee shot to fame with her book ‘To Kill a Mockingbird’ which was written in 1960. She very recently published her second book ‘Go Set a Watchman’ which reader world over welcomed with open arms.

Shaking the world with just one book, Lee gave to us a book, a thought process and very lovable characters who will, I am sure, be cherished for a long time.
Here are ten quotes from two of her books that give us a little more to think about, a little more to get inspired from and a little more to remember her by.
